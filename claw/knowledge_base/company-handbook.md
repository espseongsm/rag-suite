# Company Handbook

A short reference covering the policies Claw is most often asked about.

## Vacation policy

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per calendar year,
plus 10 paid US public holidays. Vacation accrues monthly and unused
days roll over up to a maximum of 25 days at the start of the next
year. Submit time-off requests in BambooHR at least 5 business days in
advance for any absence longer than two consecutive days.

## Sick leave

Employees may take up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year without a
doctor's note. Absences of more than three consecutive days require a
doctor's certificate uploaded to BambooHR. Sick leave does not roll
over.

## Parental leave

New parents are entitled to 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave,
which can be taken any time within the first 12 months after the
child's birth or adoption. An additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave is
available on request.

## Expense policy

Out-of-pocket expenses up to USD 250 per item can be reimbursed
without prior approval. Anything above that requires manager approval
in advance. Submit receipts in Expensify within 30 days of the
purchase. Travel bookings should always be made through TravelPerk;
direct hotel/airline bookings are reimbursable only when TravelPerk is
unavailable.

## Working hours and remote work

Standard working hours are 9:00 to 17:00 in the employee's local time
zone. Employees are free to shift this window by up to two hours with
manager agreement. Remote work is supported globally; visits to the
nearest office are required at least once per quarter.

## Equipment

A new laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse are provided on the first
day of employment. Hardware refreshes are available every three years
or earlier on manager approval. Lost or damaged equipment must be
reported to IT within one business day.
